Welcome to Secrets in Proverbs

A Cultural Studies Research Center committed to unveiling the enigmatic depths of proverbs from across the globe.

Led by developers Basit Zahoor, Ananya B N, and Hafsa Noorain, our mission is to delve into the profound cultural heritage encapsulated within these timeless expressions.

With an interdisciplinary approach, we explore the historical, linguistic, and sociological dimensions of proverbs, offering illuminating insights into diverse cultures and societies.

Embark on this captivating journey with us as we decode the mysteries concealed within proverbs.
